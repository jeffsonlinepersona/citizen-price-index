How the index works

The Citizen Price Index is a simple, transparent way to see how the cost of everyday life is changing over time.

Anyone can submit a recent purchase with the date, location, item, and price. Over time, these small contributions add up to a bottom-up picture of inflation.

  • 1
    Capture real purchases
    Record the things you actually buy: groceries, gas, services, and more.
  • 2
    Normalize & aggregate
    Data is grouped by item type, location, and time to build a composite index.
  • 3
    Track change over time
    The index shows how the overall price level moves month to month and year to year.

Your last receipt can improve the index.

It takes less than a minute to contribute. No account, no login – just the basics: date, location, item, and price.
