Contribute a purchase

Help improve the Citizen Price Index by sharing a recent purchase. You only need four things: date, location, item, and price – plus whether it was on sale.

For now, this form is a demo and does not send data anywhere. It’s here so you can design and refine the data entry flow.
Was this price on sale?

In a future version, this form can send data to a database, Google Sheet, or API. For now, it’s designed as a clean data entry experience you can iterate on.
